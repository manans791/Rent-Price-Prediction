House Rent Prediction

In urban areas, newcomers often face challenges when searching for rental accommodation. Property dealers, exploiting their unfamiliarity with local rental market rates, frequently overcharge new residents for rental properties. This exploitative practice leads to financial strain and dissatisfaction among tenants.

To address this issue, we propose the development of a Rent House Price Prediction system. This system aims to empower individuals seeking rental accommodation by providing accurate predictions of rental prices based on various property features and tenant preferences. By leveraging factors such as the number of bedrooms, property size, bathrooms, location, furnishing status, and tenant preferences, the system will offer tailored rental price estimates reflective of fair market rates.

Features for the prediction includes

  • BHK
  • Size
  • Floor
  • Bathroom
  • Furnishing status etc
  • City
